About Us

About Diaimic Independent Shared Living

We are dedicated to providing a safe, clean, and structured living environment for individuals in need of stable housing.

Diaimic Independent Shared Living serves people from many walks of life, including homeless individuals, veterans, domestic violence survivors, ex-offenders, and those receiving SSI or SSDI benefits.

Our goal is to create a respectful and peaceful shared living space where residents can feel supported, comfortable, and focused on moving forward.

What We Provide

Every resident deserves a clean, comfortable, and supportive place to live. Here is what you can expect at Diaimic Independent Shared Living.

Safe & Clean Environment

Our properties are maintained to high cleanliness standards so residents feel comfortable and secure.

Shared Living Spaces

Common areas are well-maintained and shared respectfully among residents to foster a sense of community.

Fully Furnished Rooms

Move in without the stress. Rooms are furnished and ready so you can focus on what matters most.

Utilities Included

Water, electricity, and heat are included so residents can budget predictably without surprise bills.

Drug & Alcohol-Free

We maintain a sober living environment to ensure the safety and wellbeing of all residents.

Structure & House Rules

Clear, fair house rules create a stable and respectful environment for everyone in the home.

Supportive Atmosphere

We foster a community where residents support one another and are treated with dignity and respect.
